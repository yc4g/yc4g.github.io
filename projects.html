Khushi Baby

Khushi Baby, cofounded and led by Yale alumnus Ruchit Nagar in 2014, is a non-profit devoted to providing doctors in India with a platform for organizing the medical records of mothers and newborn infants. Here at Yale with Code4Good, our tech team is working with Khushi Baby to improve upon the online dashboard used by doctors and nurses to track the health of over 10,000 mothers and children. Working with technology such as Ruby on Rails, SQL and Javascript, we’ve begun implementing features such as an active user counter and an interactive map UI.
